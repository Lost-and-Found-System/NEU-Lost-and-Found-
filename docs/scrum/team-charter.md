# 👥 Team Roles & Responsibilities

This document defines the core roles within our Scrum team to ensure accountability and clear ownership.

### 🛡️ Scrum Master
- Facilitates Scrum ceremonies (Daily Stand-ups, Sprint Planning, Retros).
- ✅ Removes blockers and impediments for the team.
- Coaches the team on Scrum values and agile practices.

### 🧠 KM Analyst (Knowledge Management)
- Manages project documentation and internal wikis.
- ✅ Ensures information flow and data accuracy across the team.
- Curates technical and process-based knowledge for future scaling.

### 🎨 UI/UX Designer
- Responsible for user research, wireframing, and high-fidelity mockups.
- ✅ Ensures the product is intuitive and visually consistent.
- Works closely with Developers to ensure design feasibility.

### 💻 Full Stack Developer
- Responsible for end-to-end feature development (Frontend & Backend).
- ✅ Writes clean, maintainable, and well-tested code.
- Participates in peer code reviews and architectural planning.

### 🔍 QA / Docu (Quality Assurance & Documentation)
- Executes manual and automated testing to ensure bug-free releases.
- ✅ Validates that all features meet the Acceptance Criteria.
- Maintains technical documentation and user manuals for the final product.

# 🤝 Working Agreements

Our team’s "rules of the road" for a healthy and productive remote environment.

### 📡 Communication
- ✅ **Primary Channel:** GMEET for quick chats; Email for formal updates.
- ✅ **Core Hours:** All members available for sync between 7:00 AM – 9:00 PM.
- ✅ **Status Updates:** Daily Stand-up happens at [Time] via Messenger.

### 🚀 Collaboration & Workflow
- **PR Turnaround:** All PRs should be reviewed within 24 hours.
- ✅ **Respect Time:** No meetings during "Deep Work" blocks (e.g., Tuesday afternoons).
- ✅ **Transparency:** If you are blocked for more than 2 hours, post in the team channel.

### 🛠️ Conflict Resolution
- We address issues directly and professionally.
- ✅ If a technical disagreement occurs, the lead role for that domain has the final say.
